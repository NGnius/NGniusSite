Technologists is an online clan made for Robocraft players who want to find others like them. We enjoy playing and discussing Robocraft along with a diverse range of other games and subjects. The Technologists clan strives for a casual, fun environment. We try to ensure that everyone feels included, and that everyone has some fun every once in a while. Due to our small size we don't organize our own events, but we sometimes attend other events.

Join our Discord Server!
