--- layout: default+ title: How to Reinstall EasyAntiCheat ---
1. Go to the EasyAntiCheat folder in wherever Robocraft is installed on your system
2. Run EasyAnitCheat_Setup.exe
3. Click Uninstall and then select Robocraft from the drop-down menu
4. Click Next twice
5. If see four green checkmarks skip to step 10. Otherwise continue with step 6
6. Close and then open EasyAntiCheat_Setup.exe again
7. Click install and then select Robocraft from the drop-down menu
8. Click Next twice
9. Close EasyAntiCheat Setup and repeat steps 2 through 5
10. Congratulations, you have reinstalled EasyAntiCheat (at least once)! Now go test the game out to see if that fixed your problem.
